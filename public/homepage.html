Protector

Our goal at Protector is to lead the way in developing cutting-edge safety solutions for both residential and business environments. Our state-of-the-art sensor system is intended to give immediate information about any gas leaks and fire threats. Our sensor, in contrast to traditional alarms, connects to a centralised platform and provides accurate information on gas concentrations that may be dangerous during fire situations. With the use of this technology, people may take proactive steps to secure their homes and workplaces by receiving important information in simply understood numbers.

The Sensor

The sensors detect gases signaling potential fire hazards, providing detailed percentage-based data. This unique feature allows users to gauge the severity: higher percentages indicate greater fire risk.

Navigating This Site

To access real-time percentage readings of hazardous gases and receive fire detection notifications, simply navigate to the dashboard.Any information received from the sensors will be promptly posted by the admin on this platform .

Dashboard

Our dashboard makes use of a percentage range of 0% to 100%. The intensity or severity of a fire hazard is shown by this range. When it is at 0%, it means that there isn't a fire hazard to be found. A higher percentage figure denotes a higher probability or severity of a fire hazard.

Navigating Fires

If a fire hazard is detected by the detection system, please remain calm and follow the alarm instructions provided by professionals at your location. Above, you'll find a link that guides you on navigating fire emergencies.
